Smart Classroom Solution

Problem Statement

Many lecture halls on campus lack projectors and smart teaching equipment, which reduces effective content delivery and student engagement.

Proposed Solution

The Smart Classroom Solution is a low-cost, portable, and easy-to-use digital teaching system that allows instructors to deliver lessons effectively without relying on expensive infrastructure.

How It Works

Teachers use a web-based interface to display digital content, slides, and videos. Students can follow along on their devices or a shared projector. The system simplifies lesson delivery and improves engagement.

Key Features

  • Low-cost setup for any classroom
  • Supports slides, videos, and images
  • Easy-to-use web interface
  • Portable and scalable
