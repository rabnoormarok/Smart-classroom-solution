Smart Classroom Solution

Interactive, low-cost, digital learning for modern classrooms

Problem Statement

Many classrooms lack projectors, smart boards, and digital tools. Students have limited engagement, and teaching becomes less effective.

Proposed Solution

Smart Classroom Solution is a web-based, low-cost system that allows teachers to deliver interactive lessons digitally. It increases engagement and improves learning outcomes.

How It Works

Teachers log into the system and display slides, videos, and quizzes. Students follow along on any device. Real-time polls and questions keep lessons interactive.
