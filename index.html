Smart Classroom Solution

Interactive, low-cost, and digital learning for modern classrooms

Problem Statement

Many lecture halls lack projectors, smart boards, and digital teaching equipment. This reduces engagement and learning quality for students.

Proposed Solution

The Smart Classroom Solution is a low-cost, portable, and easy-to-use system enabling teachers to deliver lessons digitally. It supports interactive learning and reduces dependency on expensive equipment.

How It Works

Teachers use a web interface to display slides, videos, and interactive content. Students follow along on their devices or a shared projector. Quizzes and polls engage students during lessons.
